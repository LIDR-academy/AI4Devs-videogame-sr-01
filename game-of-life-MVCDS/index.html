Conway's Game of Life

A cellular automaton devised by mathematician John Conway

5 gen/s
Generation: 0

FPS: 0

How to Play:

  • Drag to paint multiple cells
  • Select patterns from the dropdown - you'll see a blue preview shadow that shows where the pattern will be placed
  • Click to place pattern when you see the preview in the desired location
  • Use controls to play, pause, step, or generate random patterns

Rules:

  • Live cell with 2-3 neighbors survives
  • Dead cell with exactly 3 neighbors becomes alive
  • All other cells die or remain dead
  • Note: Single isolated cells will die when the game advances to the next generation (they need neighbors to survive)
  • Tip: The game automatically pauses when you edit the grid, so your changes won't disappear until you press Play
